How to Play who wants to be a millionaire

In who wants to be a millioniare?, contestants mjust answer a series of 15 multiple
choice questionsto win increasing amounts of money. There's no time limit, and
players recieve three lifelines to help with questions.
The game is divided into five difficulty levels, each containing three questions that
get progressively harder:
*              Level 1:Questions 1-3(easiest)
*              Level 2:Questions 4-6(slightly harder)
*              Level 3: Questions 7-9 (moderate difficulty)
*              Level 4: Questions 10-12 (very difficult)
*              Level 4: Questions 13-15 (most difficult)
Questions within a level share a similar difficulty but can cover a variety of topics.
The game also featyres three "safe havens" at Question 5, 10, and 15, where
contestants secure their winnings. if a player answers incorrectly before reaching a
safe haven, they risk losing a significant portion of their accumulated money.
